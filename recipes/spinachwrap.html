Spinach Wrap

The worlds best Spinach Wrap!
It is what it is, And it's the best Wrap for athletes!

Ingredients

  • 1 7-inch whole-wheat tortilla
  • 25 grams sliced mushrooms
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 450 grams fresh spinach
  • 1 large egg, plus 1 egg white, lightly whisked
  • 2 tablespoons crumbled low-fat feta cheese
  • 1 tablespoon sun-dried tomatoes, chopped

Directions

  1. Place the tortilla on a microwave-safe plate and heat on high for one minute
  2. Coat a small skillet with cooking spray, then place it over medium heat
  3. Sauté the mushrooms and pepper for two minutes
  4. Stir in the spinach and sauté for another one to two minutes, or until wilted.
  5. Add the eggs and cook, stirring often until they are set, about two minutes
  6. Place the egg scramble in the centre of the tortilla, then top it with the crumbled feta and sun-dried tomatoes
  7. To wrap, fold up one end, then both sides
  8. Serve immediately or wrap in foil and take to go
  9. Ejoy!
