Garlic Bread

The worlds best Garlic Bread!
It's very little work, And it's worth it!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup grated Romano cheese
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 loaf (1 pound) French bread, halved lengthwise
  • 2 tablespoons minced fresh parsley

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. In a small bowl, mix butter, cheese and garlic; brush over cut sides of bread
  3. Place on a baking sheet, cut sides up
  4. Sprinkle with parsley
  5. Ejoy!
